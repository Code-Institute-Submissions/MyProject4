What is Unicorn Attractor?

Unicorn attractor is an augmented reality mobile game which uses your mobile devices GPS to locate, capture and customise the virtual unicorns, which appear as if they are in the players real-world location.

How to play

Unicorn Attractor is played by using your mobile device as a special screen where only through this screen you can see these magical and beautiful creatures. If none are seen on your screen you can take a walk and try and find them or alternativly you can drop the magical unicorn food on the screen and wait for them to come to you.

Customise Unicorns

Want to have a Unicorn that looks how you want it to look?? Then look no further. Once you have found some Unicorns you can then customise them from the colour of their horn to the colour of their tail, the possibilities are endles.
